Map view

Upper left corner of the map shows a rotating animation when the view is being updated.

The spinner on the left lets you choose what is displayed on the map. You can select either trains or stations, or both at the same time. This choice does not affect the search or schedule choices.

Searches for stations or trains currently on the move. A spinner lets you confine the search to either category. You can confine the search with the text field by writing part of the name or ID. When you click a list item, the map will center on the choice. If you long click item, the schedule of the selected item will be opened.

Centers the map on your location. If you press the button longer, you can choose to view the closest train or station.

Retrieves newest train location info and refreshes the map view. On long click presents the time stamp of the last refresh. The application refreshes the data in even intervals automatically. You can change the interval in the settings.

When you click train and station markers on the map, individual information of the choice will be shown. If there is multiple markers on top of each other, a small dialog will be opened to help with the selection.
